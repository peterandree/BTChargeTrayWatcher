# Android Installation Guide — ntfy Push Notifications

This guide explains how to install the ntfy app on Android and subscribe to BTChargeTrayWatcher battery alerts.

## Prerequisites
- An Android device with Google Play or F-Droid installed.
- BTChargeTrayWatcher installed and running on your Windows PC.
- A topic generated by BTChargeTrayWatcher (see **Mobile notifications → Show topic** in the tray menu).

## Steps

### 1 — Install ntfy
**Option A — Google Play:**
1. Open Google Play on your Android device.
2. Search for **ntfy**.
3. Install the app by Philipp Heckel (package ID: `io.heckel.ntfy`).

**Option B — F-Droid:**
1. Open F-Droid.
2. Search for **ntfy**.
3. Install the app.

> The F-Droid flavor uses a direct WebSocket connection to the server instead of Firebase Cloud Messaging. This is more private and does not depend on Google services. Delivery is equally fast.

### 2 — Subscribe to your topic
1. Open the ntfy app.
2. Tap the **+** or **Subscribe** button.
3. For **Server**, enter: `ntfy.sh`
4. For **Topic**, enter the topic shown by BTChargeTrayWatcher.
   - You can find it under **Mobile notifications → Show topic** in the tray menu.
   - Example: `btcw-8h2qv6m4k9r1x7p3`
5. Tap **Subscribe**.

### 3 — Allow notifications
1. When prompted by Android, allow notifications for the ntfy app.
2. If you do not receive a prompt, go to **Android Settings → Apps → ntfy → Notifications** and enable them.

### 4 — Allow battery-unrestricted background operation (recommended)
1. Go to **Android Settings → Apps → ntfy → Battery**.
2. Select **Unrestricted**.
3. This prevents Android from killing the background connection and missing alerts.

### 5 — Enable instant delivery (Google Play flavor only)
1. Open the ntfy app settings.
2. Enable **Instant delivery via foreground service**.
3. Confirm the persistent notification that appears — this keeps the connection alive.

### 6 — Test the setup
1. Return to BTChargeTrayWatcher on your PC.
2. Open **Mobile notifications → Send test notification** in the tray menu.
3. Confirm the notification arrives on your phone within a few seconds.

## Troubleshooting
- **No notification received:** Check that the topic in the ntfy app matches exactly what BTChargeTrayWatcher shows.
- **Delayed delivery on Play flavor:** Enable instant delivery via foreground service (Step 5).
- **Delayed delivery on F-Droid:** Disable battery optimization for ntfy (Step 4).
